About me

Hi there! My name is AgnÄ— and I am a Lithuanian tattoo artist based in Berlin. How did I get here?

Once upon a time I finished my graphic design studies in Vilnius, Lithuania and thought about creating logos for a living, but life had a different plan for me and I moved to Barcelona, Spain.

Barcelonians inspired me with their passion for tattoos and soon I decided to not only get it for myself, but do it for others! After Barcelona I decided to move to Berlin - the ultimate capital of the Tattoo Art.

What's my style you may ask? I'm counting my 6th year in this tattoo business and I've dipped my toes in different styles, but eventually I found myself being fascinated the most with Irezumi, neo-traditional, ukiyo-e, flora and fauna-themed tattoos.
